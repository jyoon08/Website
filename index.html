Ramen Noodles

  • Time: Only 5 minutes!
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
ONE PACKET OF INSTANT RAMEN, WATER 1 cup of water

Step 1: BOIL WATER UNTIL BUBBLING.
Step 2: PUT IN RAMEN WITH THE POWDERS IN THE WATER
Step 3:TEST UNTIL SMOOTH. EAT!!!!!!!!

Pasta

  1. Time: 5 - 8 hours
  2. Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
cubed beef stew meat, 1 pound
condensed golden mushroom soup, 1 can (10.75 ounce)
chopped onion, 1/2 cup
worcestershire sauce, 1 Tablespoon
water 1/4 cup water,
cream cheese, 4 ounces

Step 1:In a slow cooker, combine the meat, soup, onion, Worcestershire sauce and water on the pasta.
Step 2:Cook on Low setting for 8 hours, or on High setting for about 5 hours. Stir in cream cheese just before serving.
